Description

Nestled in the quiet hills of Montenero, “La Luna nel Bosco” is a freshly renovated 125 m² apartment, on the first floor of the outbuilding of a 19th-century villa featuring a large centuries-old park, a seasonal pool and a tennis court. The location is ideal to visit Livorno, Pisa, Lucca, the Etruscan Coast and Tuscany, coming back to relax on the beautiful private panoramic terrace or, in winter, by the cozy fireplace.

What to do in and around Livorno

Curated ideas to enjoy sea, nature and flavours of the Tuscan coast. All within a short drive from La Luna nel Bosco.

  • 🌊 Sea & beaches
    • Sonnino beach – 10 min • Small cove, crystal-clear water.
    • Calafuria cliffs – 15 min • Jumps, snorkeling, iconic sunsets.
    • Castiglioncello – 20 min • Seaside walk, beach clubs and restaurants.
  • 🌿 Nature & hiking
    • Montenero trails • Paths among olive groves and Mediterranean scrub.
    • Valle Benedetta • Woods, streams and old country roads.
    • Sanctuary of Montenero • View over the whole coastline.
  • 🏛️ Culture & cities
    • Venezia district – Fortezza Nuova • Canals, evening spots, unique vibe.
    • Fattori Museum (Villa Mimbelli) • Macchiaioli and Tuscan art.
    • Port of Livorno & ferries • Boats to Capraia, Corsica, Sardinia.
  • 🍝 Local flavours
    • Central Market • Fresh produce and Livornese street food.
    • Cacciucco “alla livornese” • The city’s iconic fish stew.
    • Wine bars & traditional trattorias • Tuscan wines and seafood cuisine.
  • 🗺️ Day trips
    • Pisa – 25 min • Leaning Tower and Piazza dei Miracoli.
    • Lucca – 45 min • Renaissance walls and old town.
    • Excursions to Capraia / Gorgona • Islands of the Tuscan Archipelago Park.
Tip: in peak season bring water shoes for Calafuria and book seaside restaurants in advance.

FAQ – Frequently asked questions

What is the minimum stay at La Luna nel Bosco in Livorno?

The minimum stay is 4 nights. This allows you to fully enjoy the holiday home and take your time exploring Montenero hills, Livorno’s sea, and nearby cities such as Pisa and Lucca.

How many people can the apartment host?

The apartment comfortably hosts up to 5 people (+1 extra on request), perfect for families or small groups of friends.

Is the holiday home suitable for families with children?

Absolutely. There are generous spaces, a private terrace and a large park where kids can play (always under adult supervision).

Is the house heated in winter?

Yes, the apartment is heated and also features a cozy fireplace to create a warm, relaxing atmosphere on colder evenings.

Are pets allowed at La Luna nel Bosco?

Unfortunately not. To guarantee perfectly clean spaces and comfort for guests with allergies, pets are not allowed.

What are check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 3:00 PM onward, while check-out is by 10:00 AM. If you have special needs, let us know and we’ll try to help depending on availability.

Is the parking suitable for large vehicles or vans?

Yes, parking is spacious and accessible to vans as well. Please mention it when booking so we can organize spaces at best.

How far is the sea from La Luna nel Bosco?

The house is on the hills of Montenero, a short drive from the best beaches: 10 minutes to Sonnino, 15 to Calafuria and 20 to Castiglioncello.

How far is the port of Livorno?

About 15 minutes by car. The location is convenient if you need to catch ferries to Sardinia, Corsica or the island of Capraia.

Are supermarkets, pharmacies and services nearby?

Yes, you can reach supermarkets, pharmacies and other essentials in about 5 minutes by car. Public transport connects Montenero with Livorno’s centre.

Are there hiking routes close to the house?

Yes. Several panoramic trails start from the surroundings of Montenero and are great for nature walks.

Are there cycling or mountain bike routes?

The nearby hills offer routes for both road and mountain bikes, suitable for different ability levels.

Where to eat near La Luna nel Bosco?

Within a few minutes you’ll find traditional Tuscan trattorias, fish restaurants and wine bars with local specialties.
